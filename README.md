# 📖 Overview

Financial reporting is most valuable when it helps decision-makers quickly understand performance, profitability, and growth opportunities.

This project demonstrates how Power BI can transform transactional financial data into an executive-ready dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor sales, evaluate discount strategies, compare regional performance, and identify the products contributing most to overall business success.

The dashboard combines interactive visualizations, KPI monitoring, and financial analytics to support strategic business decisions.

# 📊 Key Business Insights

### 💰 Overall Financial Performance

- **Total Sales:** **118.73M**
- **Total Discounts:** **9.21M**
- **Total Quantity Sold:** **5,615 Units**

---

### 🏆 Product Performance

Paseo is the highest-performing product, generating significantly more sales than any other product line and outperforming its nearest competitor by a considerable margin.

---

### 🌍 International Market Distribution

Sales are well balanced across five countries:

- 🇨🇦 Canada — 22%
- 🇫🇷 France — 21%
- 🇺🇸 United States — 21%
- 🇲🇽 Mexico — 18%
- 🇩🇪 Germany — 18%

The balanced distribution indicates a diversified international customer base with no excessive dependence on a single market.

---

### 🏷 Discount Strategy

Overall discount spending remains relatively small compared to total revenue.

However, high-selling products such as **Paseo** also receive the highest total discounts, suggesting opportunities to evaluate pricing strategy without significantly impacting sales volume.
